Culture is communication and communication is culture. (Hall)
Culture is to a human collective what personality is to an individual. (Hofstede)
Culture is a group worldview, the way of organizing the world that a particular society has created over time. (Nolan) 
Shared knowledge gives rise to shared meanings, which are carried in the shared physical environment (such as the spatial layout of a rural village, subsistence economy) social institutions (e.g., schools, family, the workplace), social practices (e.g., division of labor) the language, conversation scripts, and other media (e.g., religious scriptures, cultural icons, folklores, idioms).(Chiu and Hong)
